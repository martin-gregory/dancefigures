Dance Figures Art collection by Tiana Diakova

Dance Figures Art is a browser-based experience of Tiana Diakova's conceptual figurative painting collection, where movement, choreography and the human form are translated into layered visual compositions.

The site combines large-scale artwork with a custom parallax scroll, to create an immersive experience of each piece. It is a source for dance studios, curators, and galleries seeking contemporary figurative paintings of dancers and movement-inspired figurative art.

Collection Highlights

  • Held by the Wind A conceptual painting by Tiana Diakova exploring suspended movement, gesture and shifting balance — three female figures in elegant poses with flowing air brush marks, evoking a sense of movement and freedom. Oil and acrylic on layered plastic sheets, 100 x 70 cm, 2026.
  • Moved by the Tide Three male figures in powerful poses with brush marks representing the tide as they move through the scene. A movement led figurative painting focused on flow, rhythm and motion, presented as part of the Dance Figures Art collection. Oil and acrylic on layered plastic sheets, 100 x 70 cm, 2026.
  • The Song of the Swaying Dunes I The female figures, arms open, caught in a moment of weightless suspension — the first work in a diptych series, a movement-inspired conceptual painting. Oil and acrylic on layered plastic sheets, 100 x 70 cm, 2026.
  • The Song of the Swaying Dunes II The female figures continue their movement, a celebration of the grace of the body — the second work in the dunes diptych, a movement inspired conceptual painting. Oil and acrylic on layered plastic sheets, 100 x 70 cm, 2026.
